Typography I

Challenge

The goal of this lab was to work with my partner, create a lab 13 index.html and create a fake website using typography and they knowledge I have. This lab included using multiple fonts, ids, divs, and block styling. I chose to mimic the screenshot from Mail Chimp's 2016 holiday tips page. I didn't have a partner for this lab.

For this lab I;

  • created a lab 13 folder and its corresponding files,
  • created a fake.html and a fake.css for my mimic site,
  • I found fonts similar to the screenshot from google fonts,
  • I used spans and blocks to mimic the typography,
  • created a z frame for my fake site to appear in my lab 13.

    • Problems

    Finding the fonts that looked similar to the screenshot I used was the most difficult part of the lab. Before settling on google fonts I spent hours trying to find look alikes on dafont.com and tried my best to use the font generators generated in class.

    The tricky part of displaying the typography was creating individual classes for my words and letters that used different fonts.

    After inserting my letters and words I then had to position them so that they mimic the type of my screenshot. I also had to find and make some graphics that matched the original site. Below is a screenshot of the original site.
