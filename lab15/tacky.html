Retrowave

What Is It

Retrowave is both an aesthetic, art style and music genre. Made with synth 80's themes, futurism, techno and industrial sounds retrowave is a unique robotic mashup. Much like other "wave" genres, it focuses on technological music along with dystopian futurism. The retro themes include 80's synth, 90's DnB/techno/hardcore, 80's Japanese film soundbites, and 80's future industrial vibes. Think TRON and Back To the Future mixed with techno.

Similar Genres

Synthwave is the parent of all "wave" genres. This genre is more music related and doesnt use any retro or futureistic themes. The genre focuses on using sound synthesis techniques.

Post punk wave genres include Coldwave and Minimalwave, these came from New Wave. New Wave is the oldest wave genre, originating from Europe in the 70's.

Vaporwave is the glitch offspring of New Wave and Retrowave. This wave ranges from chill tunes to hardcore using chopped and skewed techniques.
